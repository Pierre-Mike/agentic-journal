# Skills Conventions

- **SK-C001: canvas-generator supersedes json-canvas**  
  confidence: 0.7 | added: 2026-04-03  
  canvas-generator explicitly states it replaces json-canvas. Both skills still exist. Prefer canvas-generator for new Obsidian canvas work; json-canvas is legacy. Do not remove json-canvas without checking dependents.

- **SK-C002: Three overlapping skill-creation skills coexist intentionally**  
  confidence: 0.7 | added: 2026-04-03  
  write-a-skill (simple creation guide), skill-generator (full lifecycle with evals and benchmarking), and tdd-skill-creation (TDD-first with locked evals) serve different use cases. They are not duplicates — they differ in depth and methodology.
